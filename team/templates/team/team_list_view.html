Fellows

The next fellowship admissions will begin in January 2024. Fellows can remain with the firm, they do not have to reapply year on year.

Apply for a fellowship via email to collective@collective.vc.

Use the subject line “Fellowship Application *Your Name*” or your application will not be considered.

There is no recommended format, as we want you to pitch yourself however you see fit. We do however ask that you write a biography and a photograph suitable for our website and social media along with your application, and specify which fellowship category you are applying for.

Fellowships are crucial to our operation. Currently, fellowships are offered in the following categories: Technology, Science, Healthcare, Sustainability, Empowerment, Infrastructure, Development and Finance. Fellowships are currently unpaid, similar to an internship, but this will be under constant review as the company develops in order to incentivise and attract high-quality talent, as well as compensate fellows for their input. Fellows can progress to partners after demonstrating a strong track record of investment selection, subject specific advice, and respective investment performance.
